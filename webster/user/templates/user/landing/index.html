About Webster

Webster is a service app that helps people to maintain a website for their business needs with just an Instagram account. Anyone with an Instagram account can download our app and register the account with us. The client can then advertise their products through their Instagram posts as usual and we scrape it from the profile of the client, and generate a website with a template selected by the client. Everytime the client updates his insta account with a new product it is automatically added to the website, and users can purchase it from the site.
